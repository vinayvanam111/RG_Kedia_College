Library

At present the library has more than 10000 books on various academic and general subjects and this college is assisted by U.G.C.

All books borrowed form library will have to be returned before the issue of Hall tickets. Other wise NO DUES CERTIFICATE will nor be issued.

Books will be lent to 2nd and 3rd year students. Only after clearing the dues of previous years.

Issue of Books: Books are issued to the students as per the notified time table. For return of the books the same notified time table is applicable. Detailed information regarding issue of books can be had from the librarian.
